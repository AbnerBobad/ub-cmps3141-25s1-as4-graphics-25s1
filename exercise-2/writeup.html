Exercise-2 Writeup

Color Improvements (60-30-10 Rule)

  1. Dominant Background (60%): I changed the background from whitesmoke to a soft blue-gray (#f0f4f8) to create a calm, professional atmosphere that doesn't strain the eyes.
  2. Secondary Navbar (30%): I replaced the flat dark navbar with a deep blue gradient (navy to royal blue) to add visual depth and establish a strong brand presence.
  3. Accent Buttons (10%): I changed the black button to a teal gradient with a smooth hover effect and shadow, making call-to-action elements pop and feel more interactive.
